<p><strong>The Integrated Electric Brake Booster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Box Solution</li><li>One-Box Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Electric Brake Booster market includes two primary types: Two-Box and One-Box solutions. The Two-Box solution separates the brake booster and control unit, offering flexibility and redundancy, often used in hybrid systems. The One-Box solution integrates both components into a single unit, reducing size, weight, and cost while improving efficiency, commonly adopted in fully electric vehicles. Both solutions enhance braking performance, energy recovery, and safety, catering to the growing demand for advanced braking systems in modern vehicles.</p></p>
