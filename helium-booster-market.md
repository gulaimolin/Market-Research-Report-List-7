<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Helium Booster market includes two main types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic boosters utilize compressed air to increase helium pressure, offering advantages in remote locations with compressed air availability. In contrast, electric boosters employ electric power to efficiently compress helium, providing more precise control and often higher output rates. Both types cater to diverse applications, from industrial processes to research settings, balancing factors like energy source availability, operational efficiency, and maintenance needs to meet varying user requirements.</p></p>
