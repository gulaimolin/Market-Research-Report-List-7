<p><ul><li>Vertical Check Valve</li><li>Horizontal Check Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lift check valve market comprises primarily two types: vertical and horizontal check valves. Vertical check valves are designed to operate with the valve body oriented vertically, allowing fluid flow to lift the disc off its seat, which helps prevent backflow. Conversely, horizontal check valves are installed with the body positioned horizontally, accommodating a different flow dynamic. Both types are essential in various applications for ensuring one-directional fluid movement, enhancing system efficiency, and maintaining pressure integrity in pipelines.</p></p>
