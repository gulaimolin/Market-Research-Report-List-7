<p><p>Step drives in the market can be categorized into two main types: DC drives and AC drives. DC drives control the speed and torque of DC motors by varying the supply voltage, offering precise control for applications requiring high torque at low speeds. In contrast, AC drives manage the operation of AC motors, utilizing variable frequency to adjust speed and improve energy efficiency, making them suitable for a range of industrial applications. Both types enhance performance and energy savings in motor-driven systems.</p></p>
