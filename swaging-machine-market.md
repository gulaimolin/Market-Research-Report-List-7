<p><ul><li>Radial Swaging Machine</li><li>Rotary Swaging Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swaging machine market is primarily categorized into two types: radial swaging machines and rotary swaging machines. Radial swaging machines utilize a radial motion to compress and shape metal at a specific point, allowing for intricate designs and tighter tolerances. In contrast, rotary swaging machines employ a rotary motion to progressively shape metal, increasing efficiency for larger production runs. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive and aerospace, delivering precision in metal forming processes.</p></p>
