<p><ul><li>On-Wafer Microwave Measurement</li><li>De-embedding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Analyzers market comprises two primary types: On-Wafer Microwave Measurement and De-embedding. On-Wafer Microwave Measurement focuses on evaluating the performance of semiconductor devices directly on the wafer, providing real-time insights into electrical characteristics, enabling rapid testing and optimization. De-embedding, on the other hand, involves removing the effects of test fixtures and measurement systems from the collected data to isolate the device's intrinsic performance. Both processes are crucial for ensuring high-quality, efficient semiconductor development and production.</p></p>
