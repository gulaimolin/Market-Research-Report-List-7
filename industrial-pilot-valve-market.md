<p><p>The industrial pilot valve market is categorized into two main types: low pressure and high pressure pilot valves. Low pressure pilot valves typically operate in systems with lower pressure ranges, ensuring smooth control of fluid flow in applications like water and air systems. In contrast, high pressure pilot valves are designed for environments requiring robust performance at elevated pressure levels, often used in oil, gas, and chemical industries. Both types play critical roles in automation and process control.</p></p>
