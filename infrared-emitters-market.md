<p><p>The infrared emitters market is segmented into two primary types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and SMT (Surface Mount Technology) emitters. SMD emitters are compact and designed for direct mounting onto circuit boards, enhancing assembly efficiency and saving space. SMT emitters, while similar, refer to the broader technology used to mount components onto surfaces, offering flexibility in design and manufacturing. Both types are crucial in applications such as remote controls, sensing devices, and various communication technologies where infrared signals are essential.</p></p>
