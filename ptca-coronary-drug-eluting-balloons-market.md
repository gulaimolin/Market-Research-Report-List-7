<p><p>The PTCA (Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty) market is segmented into Drug-Eluting Balloons (DEBs) and standard balloons. DEBs are advanced devices coated with medication to prevent restenosis by releasing drugs directly at the treatment site, offering enhanced therapeutic benefits. Standard balloons, on the other hand, are simpler devices used to mechanically dilate stenosed coronary arteries without the added pharmacological effect. Each type serves specific clinical needs, with DEBs increasingly preferred for complex cases requiring prolonged therapeutic intervention.</p></p>
