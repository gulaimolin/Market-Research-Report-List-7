<p><strong>The Liquid Waterborne Printing Inks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexography Inks</li><li>Gravure Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid waterborne printing inks market primarily includes flexography inks and gravure inks. Flexography inks are designed for high-speed printing on various substrates, using a flexible relief plate, making them ideal for packaging materials. Gravure inks, on the other hand, utilize an intaglio printing process, allowing for fine detail and vibrant colors, suited for long print runs on flexible media, such as labels and wallpaper. Both types emphasize eco-friendliness and low volatile organic compound emissions.</p></p>
