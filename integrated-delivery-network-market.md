<p><p>Integrated Delivery Networks (IDNs) are structured to enhance healthcare delivery efficiency. Vertical integration involves merging organizations along the healthcare supply chain, such as hospitals acquiring outpatient clinics or pharmacies, ensuring coordinated services and streamlined patient care. In contrast, horizontal integration refers to consolidating entities at the same level, like hospitals merging with other hospitals, aiming to broaden service offerings and increase market share. Both types seek to improve patient outcomes and operational efficiencies within the healthcare system.</p></p>
