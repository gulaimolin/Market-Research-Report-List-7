<p><p>The Intra Aortic Counterpulsation Device (IACD) is a mechanical cardiac assist device designed to enhance the heart's output and improve blood flow during cardiac procedures. By inflating and deflating a balloon placed in the aorta in sync with the heart's rhythm, the IACD reduces the workload on the heart and increases coronary blood flow, making it beneficial for patients with heart failure or undergoing coronary artery bypass grafting.</p><p>The IACD market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing cardiovascular diseases, advancements in medical technology, and a rising aging population. The growing emphasis on minimally invasive procedures is also contributing to the demand for IACDs, as they allow for better patient outcomes with reduced recovery times. Additionally, innovations in IACD technology, such as improved balloon design and automated systems, are enhancing device efficacy and expanding their applications.</p><p>The Intra Aortic Counterpulsation Device Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.20% during the forecast period.
