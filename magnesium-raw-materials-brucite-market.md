<p><p>Magnesium raw materials, particularly brucite, are categorized into two primary market types: bulk and fibrous. Conversely, the fibrous type consists of finer, more specialized forms of brucite that find uses in advanced applications like pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and high-performance materials, benefiting from their unique physical and chemical characteristics.</p></p>
