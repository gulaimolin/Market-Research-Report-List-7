<p><p>The chemical indicator inks market can be categorized into three main types: solvent-based, water-based, and UV-cured inks. Solvent-based inks utilize organic solvents for superior adhesion and durability, making them suitable for various surfaces. Water-based inks are environmentally friendly, offering easier cleanup and lower VOC emissions, ideal for sustainable applications. UV-cured inks dry rapidly under ultraviolet light, providing a glossy finish and high resistance to abrasion, making them popular for high-speed printing processes and specific industrial uses.</p></p>
