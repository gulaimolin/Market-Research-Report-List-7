<p><ul><li>PPG Backbone</li><li>PEG Backbone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyether Diamines market is primarily categorized into two types based on the backbone used: PPG (Polypropylene Glycol) Backbone and PEG (Polyethylene Glycol) Backbone. PPG backbone diamines are known for their excellent flexibility and chemical resistance, making them suitable for applications in coatings and adhesives. In contrast, PEG backbone diamines offer enhanced hydrophilicity and lower toxicity, making them ideal for pharmaceuticals and biomaterials. Each type caters to specific industrial requirements, driving their demand in various sectors.</p></p>
