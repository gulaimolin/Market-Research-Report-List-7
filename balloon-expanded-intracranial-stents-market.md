<p><p>The balloon-expanded intracranial stents market is divided into two main types: open-loop stents and closed-loop stents. Open-loop stents feature an unconnected design, allowing flexibility and adaptability in navigating cerebral arteries, making them suitable for various anatomical configurations. Conversely, closed-loop stents have a connected structure that offers enhanced stability and support in maintaining vessel patency. Both types are crucial in treating conditions like intracranial aneurysms and stenosis, catering to different clinical needs and procedural requirements.</p></p>
