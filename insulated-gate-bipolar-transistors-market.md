<p><p>The Insulated-Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) market consists of two primary types: Discrete IGBTs and IGBT Modules. Discrete IGBTs are individual semiconductor devices that control electrical power and are commonly used in applications like inverters and motor drives. In contrast, IGBT Modules combine multiple IGBTs and other components into a single unit for higher power handling and efficiency, making them ideal for industrial applications and renewable energy systems. Both types are essential for efficient power management in various sectors.</p></p>
