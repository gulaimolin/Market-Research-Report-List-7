<p><ul><li>Mechanical Radiator Fan</li><li>Electric Radiator Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle cooling fans market comprises two main types: mechanical radiator fans and electric radiator fans. Mechanical radiator fans are typically driven by the engine, using a belt to operate, and function effectively at high speeds but may be less efficient at lower speeds. In contrast, electric radiator fans operate independently of the engine, allowing for better energy efficiency and precise cooling control. The choice between these types affects vehicle performance, fuel efficiency, and overall temperature management.</p></p>
