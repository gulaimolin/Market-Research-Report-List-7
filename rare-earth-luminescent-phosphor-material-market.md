<p><p>The Rare Earth Luminescent Phosphor Material market encompasses various types based on their luminescent properties. Photoluminescence involves materials that absorb photons and re-emit them as light, typically used in displays and lighting. Electroluminescence refers to materials that emit light in response to an electric current, common in LEDs. Radiation luminescence results from exposure to radiation, useful in safety and diagnostic applications. Chemiluminescence produces light through chemical reactions, often seen in glow sticks. Other types may include thermoluminescence and mechanoluminescence.</p></p>
