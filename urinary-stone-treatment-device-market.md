<p><p>The urinary stone treatment device market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes. Ureteral stents are flexible tubes inserted to facilitate urine drainage and prevent blockages caused by stones. Nephrolithotomy involves surgical removal of larger stones from the kidney, often recommended when less invasive methods are ineffective. Lithotripsy uses focused shock waves to break stones into smaller fragments, allowing easier passage. Together, these methods offer comprehensive solutions for managing urinary stones, catering to varying patient needs and stone sizes.</p></p>
