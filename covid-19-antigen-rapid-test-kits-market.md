<p><p>The COVID-19 antigen rapid test kits market includes various sampling types: nasopharyngeal swabs, nasal swabs, oropharyngeal swabs, and saliva swabs. Nasopharyngeal swabs collect samples from deep in the nasal passage, providing high sensitivity. Nasal swabs gather samples from the front of the nose, being less invasive. Oropharyngeal swabs target the throat area, while saliva swabs offer a non-invasive alternative, making testing easier for patients. Each method serves to detect the presence of the virus quickly and efficiently.</p></p>
