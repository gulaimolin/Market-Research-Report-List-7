<p><p>The medical air sterilizer market comprises two primary types: portable air sterilizers and stand-alone air sterilizers. Portable air sterilizers are compact units designed for mobility, allowing easy use in various healthcare settings like ambulances or patient rooms. Stand-alone air sterilizers, on the other hand, are larger, fixed installations typically found in operating rooms or hospital wards, providing a higher volume of air treatment and ensuring a sterile environment over a broader area. Both types are crucial for infection control and patient safety.</p></p>
