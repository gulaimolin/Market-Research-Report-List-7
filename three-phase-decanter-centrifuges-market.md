<p><p>Three-phase decanter centrifuges are vital in separating solid, liquid, and liquid phases in various industries. The vertical decanter centrifuge features a vertical orientation that enhances sedimentation efficiency and is ideal for handling high-viscosity materials. In contrast, the horizontal decanter centrifuge operates with a horizontal rotating drum, offering superior separation of fine particles and greater throughput, making it suitable for applications in wastewater treatment, food processing, and petrochemicals. Both types optimize separation processes based on specific operational needs.</p></p>
