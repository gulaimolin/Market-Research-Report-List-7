<p><p>The multi-stage spray drying equipment market includes several types characterized by their atomization methods. Rotary atomizers utilize centrifugal force to produce fine droplets, while nozzle atomizers rely on high-pressure fluid to create sprays. Fluidized systems enhance drying efficiency by maintaining particles in a suspended state. Closed-loop systems recirculate air to minimize energy consumption. Centrifugal atomizers use rotational force for consistent particle size, while "others" encompass specialized methods tailored for unique applications, ensuring versatility in various industries.</p></p>
