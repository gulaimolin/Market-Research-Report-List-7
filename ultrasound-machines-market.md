<p><strong>The Ultrasound Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Mode</li><li>B-Mode or 2D Mode</li><li>C-Mode</li><li>M-Mode</li><li>Doppler Mode</li><li>Pulse Inversion Mode</li><li>Harmonic Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound machines market is diverse, encompassing various modes for different imaging needs. A-Mode displays amplitude information, primarily for ocular measurements. B-Mode, or 2D Mode, provides cross-sectional images, widely used in diagnostics. C-Mode offers a specialized view of tissue layers. M-Mode captures motion over time, useful in assessing heart function. Doppler Mode evaluates blood flow, while Pulse Inversion Mode enhances image quality. Harmonic Mode improves resolution by using higher frequency signals, advancing diagnostic imaging capabilities across medical applications.</p></p>
