<p><p>Aerogel insulation is categorized mainly into three types: silica, carbon, and oxides. Silica aerogels are known for their excellent thermal insulation properties and low density, making them ideal for various applications. Carbon aerogels, with their high electrical conductivity and thermal resistance, are typically used in specialized applications like electronics and aerospace. Oxide aerogels, featuring various metal oxides, are valued for enhanced thermal stability and unique properties such as hydrophobicity, catering to specific industrial needs. Each type serves distinct roles in insulation and material science.</p></p>
