<p><p>The animal feed protease market is categorized into three main types: endopeptidases, exopeptidases, and others. Endopeptidases act on peptide bonds within protein chains, breaking them down into shorter peptides, which enhances protein digestibility. Exopeptidases, on the other hand, remove terminal amino acids from peptides, facilitating their absorption. The “others” category includes specialized enzymes with unique functions or applications, further diversifying the market. These proteases play a crucial role in improving nutrient availability and overall animal feed efficiency.</p></p>
