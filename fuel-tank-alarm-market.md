<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Type</li><li>Float Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fuel Tank Alarm Market primarily comprises two types: Ultrasonic and Float type alarms. Ultrasonic alarms use sound waves to detect fuel levels, providing accurate measurements and quick response times, making them ideal for various applications. In contrast, Float type alarms utilize a buoyant device that rises and falls with the fuel level, triggering alerts when thresholds are breached. Both offer distinct advantages tailored to different operational needs, enhancing safety and efficiency in fuel management systems.</p></p>
