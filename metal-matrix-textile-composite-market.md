Fiber Glass composites offer good strength-to-weight ratios and are cost-effective, making them suitable for a range of applications. Aramid fibers are known for their high tensile strength and resistance to heat and abrasion, making them ideal for protective gear and aerospace applications. Carbon Fiber composites provide superior strength, stiffness, and lightweight properties, commonly used in automotive, aerospace, and sporting goods industries.</p></p>
