<p><p>The microdisplay market comprises several key technologies: OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diodes) provides vibrant colors and high contrast, making it ideal for wearables and AR applications; LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) offers high resolution and is commonly used in projectors and head-mounted displays; LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) remains popular for its affordability and decent performance in displays; DLP (Digital Light Processing) employs micro-mirrors for precise color and light control, widely utilized in projectors and large displays. Each type serves unique applications based on performance and cost.</p></p>
