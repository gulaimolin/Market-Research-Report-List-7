<p><p>The Real Time Tracking System market comprises various technologies, each suited for specific applications. RFID utilizes radio waves for short-range tracking of items. Wi-Fi leverages existing networks for location tracking in larger areas. Ultrasound uses sound waves, ideal for indoor precision. Infrared employs light for close-range tracking, while ZigBee offers low-power, low-data rate communication for sensor networks. Ultra-Wide Band (UWB) provides high accuracy over short distances. Other emerging technologies enrich the market, enhancing capabilities and applications across industries.</p></p>
