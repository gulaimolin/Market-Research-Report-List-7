<p><p>UHT processing machines for dairy products are designed to extend shelf life and maintain quality. There are two primary types: direct and indirect UHT processing machines. Direct UHT processing involves rapidly heating milk or cream by injecting steam or using hot water, which ensures quick temperature rise and sterilization. Indirect UHT processing uses heat exchangers, where products are heated through metal surfaces, minimizing contact with direct heat. Both methods effectively achieve long-duration preservation while retaining essential nutrients and flavor.</p></p>
