<p><p>The dive cylinder valve market consists of two primary applications: Low Pressure (LP) cylinders, operating below 250 bar, and High Pressure (HP) cylinders, functioning at 250 bar and above. LP valves are typically used in recreational diving, offering ease of use and safety in shallow waters. In contrast, HP valves are essential for professional diving and technical applications, providing higher storage pressure for extended dives and demanding environments. Both types ensure reliable gas delivery and are crucial for diver safety and performance.</p></p>
