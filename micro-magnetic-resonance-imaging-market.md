<p><ul><li>Open MRI</li><li>Closed MRI</li><li>Standard Bore MRI</li><li>Wide-Bore MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro Magnetic Resonance Imaging (μMRI) encompasses various types tailored for specific imaging needs. Open MRI systems offer a less claustrophobic experience, suitable for anxious patients. Closed MRI machines provide high-resolution images but can be restrictive. Standard Bore MRI is the traditional type with a narrow opening, ideal for routine scans. Wide-Bore MRI combines the benefits of closed systems with a larger opening for patient comfort, while maintaining image quality, making it a versatile option in clinical settings.</p></p>
