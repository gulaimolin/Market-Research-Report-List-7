<p><p>The High Energy Medical Cyclotron market is divided into two types: Single Particle Cyclotrons and Multi-Particle Cyclotrons. Single Particle Cyclotrons are designed to accelerate a single type of charged particle, typically protons, for medical applications such as cancer treatment and radioisotope production. In contrast, Multi-Particle Cyclotrons can accelerate multiple types of particles, including protons and heavier ions, allowing for advanced therapies and broader applications in nuclear medicine. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing medical imaging and treatment capabilities.</p></p>
