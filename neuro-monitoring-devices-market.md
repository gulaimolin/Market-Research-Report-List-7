<p><strong>The Neuro Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electroencephalogram</li><li>Electromyography</li><li>Transcranial Doppler</li><li>Intracranial Pressure Monitor</li><li>Cerebral Oximeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neuro monitoring devices encompass various tools used to assess and monitor brain and nervous system activities. Electroencephalogram (EEG) devices measure electrical activity in the brain, aiding in the diagnosis of seizures and other neurological disorders. Electromyography (EMG) assesses muscle function by measuring electrical impulses in muscles, useful in diagnosing neuromuscular conditions. Transcranial Doppler monitors blood flow in the brain, while intracranial pressure monitors gauge pressure within the skull. Cerebral oximeters measure oxygen levels in brain tissue, crucial for managing brain health.</p></p>
