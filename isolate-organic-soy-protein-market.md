<p><p>The isolate organic soy protein market encompasses various types, primarily including soy protein concentrates and soy protein flour. Soy protein concentrates are produced by removing most of the non-protein components from defatted soybeans, resulting in a product that is approximately 70-80% protein. These are often used in food products to enhance protein content while improving texture. Soy protein flour, on the other hand, contains all components of the soybean and typically has a lower protein concentration, ideal for baking and enriching cereals.</p></p>
