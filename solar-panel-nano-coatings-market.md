<p><p>The solar panel nano coatings market is primarily categorized into hydrophilic and hydrophobic types. Hydrophilic coatings attract water, promoting a self-cleaning effect by allowing dirt and debris to wash off easily with rain. This enhances the efficiency of solar panels by maintaining surface cleanliness. In contrast, hydrophobic coatings repel water, forming a protective layer that prevents moisture accumulation and reduces the risk of corrosion. Both types aim to enhance panel performance and longevity, catering to diverse environmental conditions.</p></p>
