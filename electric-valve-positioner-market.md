<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioner</li><li>Double Acting Positioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Valve Positioner market is segmented into single-acting and double-acting positioners. Single-acting positioners control valves with a spring return mechanism, providing actuation in one direction, ideal for applications requiring fail-safe operation. In contrast, double-acting positioners utilize power in both directions to achieve more precise control and faster response times, suitable for complex processes. Both types enhance valve performance and accuracy, catering to various industrial applications and improving overall operational efficiency across sectors such as oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
