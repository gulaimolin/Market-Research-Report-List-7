<p><p>The solvent cement market is categorized into various types based on the resin material used. PVC solvent cement is primarily used for joining polyvinyl chloride pipes and fittings, offering strong bonds and resistance to chemicals. CPVC solvent cement is designed for chlorinated polyvinyl chloride, suitable for higher temperature applications, like hot water systems. ABS solvent cement is used for joining acrylonitrile-butadiene-styrene pipes, known for its high impact resistance and flexibility. Each type serves distinct industrial applications in plumbing and construction.</p></p>
