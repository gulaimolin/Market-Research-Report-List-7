<p><ul><li>Heated Roll Laminater</li><li>Cold Roll Laminater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roll laminators market is divided into two primary types: heated roll laminators and cold roll laminators. Heated roll laminators utilize heat to activate adhesive and bond the laminate to the substrate, providing a durable finish suitable for various applications. In contrast, cold roll laminators use pressure without heat, making them ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Both types serve distinct purposes in commercial and industrial settings, catering to different laminating needs based on material compatibility and desired outcome.</p></p>
