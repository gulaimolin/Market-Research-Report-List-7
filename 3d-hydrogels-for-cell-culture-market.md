<p><p>The 3D hydrogels for cell culture market is categorized into scaffold-free and scaffold-based systems. Scaffold-free hydrogels rely on self-assembly techniques, allowing cells to grow in a more natural environment without predefined structures, enhancing cell-to-cell interactions. In contrast, scaffold-based hydrogels incorporate physical structures that support cell attachment and growth, promoting tissue formation. Both types offer unique advantages for research and regenerative medicine, enabling improved modeling of human tissues and more effective drug testing.</p></p>
