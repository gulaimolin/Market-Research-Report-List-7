<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Inspection Equipment Market features key technologies such as Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) and Automatic X-ray Inspection (AXI). AOI employs high-resolution cameras to visually detect defects on the PCB surface, ensuring quality control during production. In contrast, AXI utilizes X-ray imaging to inspect hidden solder joints and components beneath the surface, enabling the detection of issues that AOI might miss. Together, these technologies enhance PCB reliability and manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
