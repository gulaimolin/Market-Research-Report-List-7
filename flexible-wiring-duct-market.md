<p><ul><li>Slotted Wire Ducts</li><li>Solid Wall Wire Ducts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible wiring duct market consists of two primary types: slotted wire ducts and solid wall wire ducts. Slotted wire ducts feature openings or slots along their length, allowing for easy access and organization of wires while promoting airflow, ideal for applications requiring frequent cable changes. Solid wall wire ducts, on the other hand, provide a more protective enclosure, offering superior durability and shielding against physical damage, making them suitable for environments that require enhanced cable management and protection.</p></p>
