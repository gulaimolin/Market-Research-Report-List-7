<p><p>The absorbent surgical dressing market is categorized into several types. Primary wound dressings serve as the initial layer directly in contact with the wound, promoting healing. Secondary dressings support primary dressings and provide additional protection. Absorbents are designed to manage exudate, preventing leakage and infection. Bandages secure dressings in place, while adhesive tapes enhance adhesion and protect the wound from contaminants. Protectives shield the wound area from external factors, contributing to a holistic wound care approach and improving patient recovery.</p></p>
