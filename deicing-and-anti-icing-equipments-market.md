<p><ul><li>Mechanical De-icing Systems</li><li>Electric Pulse De-icing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deicing and anti-icing equipment market includes two primary types: mechanical de-icing systems and electric pulse de-icing systems. Mechanical de-icing systems rely on physical means, such as brushes and scrapers, to remove ice and snow from surfaces, ensuring operational safety. In contrast, electric pulse de-icing systems utilize high-voltage electric pulses to break the bond between ice and surfaces, allowing for efficient melting without physical contact. Both systems are essential for maintaining infrastructure and enhancing safety in cold weather conditions.</p></p>
