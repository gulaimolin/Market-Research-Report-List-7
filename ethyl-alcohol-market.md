<p><p>Ethyl alcohol, also known as ethanol, is a clear, colorless liquid with the chemical formula C₂H₅OH. It is widely used as a solvent, disinfectant, fuel additive, and in the production of alcoholic beverages. Additionally, the rising consumption of alcoholic beverages and the use of ethanol in industrial solvents further propel market expansion.</p><p>Emerging trends in the ethyl alcohol market include advancements in production technologies, such as cellulosic ethanol, which utilizes non-food biomass, reducing reliance on food crops. Overall, the market is poised for steady expansion, driven by diverse applications and evolving consumer demands.</p></p>
