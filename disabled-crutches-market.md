<p><p>The disabled crutches market includes various types designed for different needs. Forearm crutches, featuring cuffs around the forearms, offer better stability and are ideal for long-term use. Underarm or axilla crutches provide support under the arms, commonly used during recovery. Platform crutches feature a support platform for the forearm, suitable for users with wrist injuries. Leg support crutches allow individuals to rest a leg while mobilizing, catering to those with specific disabilities. Each type enhances mobility and independence.</p></p>
