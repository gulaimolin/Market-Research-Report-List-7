<p><p>The Engineered Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) market is segmented into two primary types: foam and elastomer. Foam TPUs are lightweight and provide excellent cushioning, making them ideal for applications in furniture, automotive seating, and protective padding. Elastomer TPUs, on the other hand, are known for their durability and flexibility. They are widely used in manufacturing products such as hoses, seals, and automotive components, where resistance to abrasion and extreme conditions is essential. Both types cater to diverse industries and applications.</p></p>
