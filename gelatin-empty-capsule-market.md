<p><p>The gelatin empty capsule market is primarily divided into two types: hard gelatin capsules and soft gelatin capsules. Hard gelatin capsules consist of two rigid halves that can be filled with powdered substances, making them ideal for pharmaceutical and nutraceutical formulations. Soft gelatin capsules, on the other hand, are more flexible and contain liquid or semi-solid fills, offering advantages in bioavailability and taste masking. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs in medication delivery and dietary supplements.</p></p>
