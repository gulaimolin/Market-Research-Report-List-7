<p><p>The Special Mortar market comprises two main types: Dry Special Mortar and Wet Mixed Special Mortar. In contrast, Wet Mixed Special Mortar is prepared by mixing components directly at the construction site, allowing for real-time adjustments in consistency and properties. Both types cater to diverse construction needs, providing tailored solutions for various applications.</p></p>
