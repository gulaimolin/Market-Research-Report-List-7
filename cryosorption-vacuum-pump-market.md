<p><p>Cryosorption vacuum pumps utilize cryogenic technology to achieve ultra-low pressures by trapping gases and vapors on cold surfaces. The market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps** use sliding vanes for gas compression.</p><p>2. **Diaphragm Vacuum Pumps** employ a flexible diaphragm for gas movement.</p><p>3. **Liquid Ring Vacuum Pumps** create a seal with a liquid ring for gas compression.</p><p>4. **Scroll Vacuum Pumps** utilize two interleaved spirals for gas transport.</p><p>5. **Turbomolecular Vacuum Pumps** rely on high-speed rotor blades to accelerate gas molecules, ideal for ultra-high vacuum applications.</p></p>
