<p><p>The freewheel clutches market comprises three primary types: sprag type, roller type, and ball bearing type. Sprag type clutches utilize sprags to engage and disengage, providing efficient torque transmission and minimal backlash. Roller type clutches employ cylindrical rollers to enable smooth operation under load, ideal for applications requiring high speed and torque. Ball bearing type clutches use balls for engagement, offering low friction and high efficiency. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, enhancing performance across various mechanical systems.</p></p>
