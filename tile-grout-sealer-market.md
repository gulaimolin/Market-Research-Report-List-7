<p><ul><li>Teflon Based</li><li>Nano Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tile & Grout Sealer market features two primary types: Teflon-based and nano-based sealers. Teflon-based sealers utilize polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) to create a chemical barrier, offering strong resistance to stains and moisture. They are ideal for high-traffic areas. On the other hand, nano-based sealers contain microscopic particles that penetrate deeper into the grout, providing superior protection and durability. These sealers enhance surface appearance while repelling dirt and water, making them suitable for both residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
