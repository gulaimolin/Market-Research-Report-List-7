<p><ul><li>Splint</li><li>Support Strap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thumb braces market comprises two primary types: splints and support straps. Splints are rigid devices designed to immobilize and stabilize the thumb, providing protection from injuries or conditions like sprains and arthritis. They often feature adjustable components for a customized fit. Support straps, on the other hand, offer flexible support and compression, aiding in pain relief while allowing for varying degrees of thumb mobility. Both types cater to individuals seeking relief from thumb-related ailments and help facilitate recovery.</p></p>
